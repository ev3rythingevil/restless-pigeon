My Coding History

On a whim and with some helpful advice from a friend, I decided to try and learn to code. This lead me to places like Coursera and Codepen. This was the start of my coding journey. Learning a new skill while also trying to balance work and a social life hasn't been easy, but the excitement that it has brought is well worth the effort. Over the course of several months, I went from having zero understanding of computers to suddenly understanding tech for the first time in my life. The doors that have been opened because of this new knowledge will lead me to greater heights!
